FAQ

Questions? Answers.

Everything you need to know before you step into the forge.

What's included in the free trial?▼
Your 7-day free trial gives you full access to our gym floor, all group classes, locker & shower facilities, and a complimentary fitness assessment with one of our coaches. No credit card required — just show up and train.
Can I freeze or cancel my membership?▼
Yes — you can freeze your membership anytime for up to 3 months at no cost. Cancellation is easy with 30 days notice, no fees, no paperwork. Month-to-month plans can be cancelled anytime.
What equipment do you have?▼
We feature 8 Olympic lifting platforms with Eleiko barbells and plates, a full selectorized machine line, over 30 cardio machines, dedicated functional training space with rigs and sleds, a 40-bike Ride Studio with immersive lighting, and a full boxing ring with 20 heavy bags.
Do you offer nutrition coaching?▼
Yes — all Forge and Elite members get a nutrition starter guide. Elite members receive a fully customized nutrition plan with weekly check-ins from our registered dietitians.
Is there parking?▼
Yes — we have a dedicated parking garage with 200+ spots, plus bike racks and easy access to Muni and BART. We're located at 742 Forge Street, just off Market.
